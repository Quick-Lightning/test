The L.N.Birla Debate initially started its journey as a local debate in 1994 wherein it was restricted to the schools of Kolkata. The tremendous response it evoked enthused the management to diversify the reach and the debate took on a Pan India dimension. The Inter Institutional L. N. Birla National Debate as its nomenclature suggests traversed the length and breadth of India. In the north, students from schools and colleges of Jaipur, Lucknow and Delhi pitted their arguments against each other while their counter parts in the south did so in Coimbatore, Bengaluru, Hyderabad and Chennai. The western zone was represented by the speakers of Pune, Nagpur and Mumbai whereas the battle of wits in the eastern zone was fought in Ranchi, Bhubaneshwar, Jamshedpur and of course the home ground Kolkata. The L.N.Birla Inter Institutional Debate conducted zone wise across the country culminated with the finals in Kolkata. Birla Debate in its new avatar is streamlined and caters to the eminent schools of Kolkata and is counted among the most prestigious debates of a city which is well known for its passion for the spoken word. The keenly fought contest results in prizes that further open the windows of knowledge for they take the form of student-friendly tech gadgets like tabs and laptops besides the cash bonanza sponsored by Syt.S.K.Birla and an attractive gift hamper. The best speaker and the best school team bag the coveted trophies
